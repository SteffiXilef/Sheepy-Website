Who is MrSuicideSheep?

MrSuicideSheep one of the best parts of YouTube. One of the purely music-based channels, track after track shows up and it is always quality. Whether it’s electro, drum & bass, dubstep, ambient/chillout, experimental, indie rock and even progressive house, it’s good. The gorgeous art used in backgrounds adds to the presentation as well. Really cool person, with a great story & they deserve all the success. Mixes get added from time to time, like the chill-out one I’ve linked below.

Why did he choose that name?


"I got the name from the PC game Worms. I used to play it a lot as a kid. One of the weapons you could use was a sheep that blew up. I made up the name SuicideSheep and stuck with it ever since."


"Ich habe den Namen von dem PC- Spiel 'Worms'. Dieses habe ich früher als Kind oft gespielt. Eine der Waffen, die man benutzen konnte, war ein Schaf das sich in die Luft sprengt. Ich erfand den Namen SuicideSheep und bin diesem seither treu geblieben."
